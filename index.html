About

Walking Alongside
Young Adults

I am a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with a passion for walking alongside college-aged young adults as they navigate one of the most formative seasons of life. The transition into adulthood can bring excitement, uncertainty, identity questions, academic pressure, relationship challenges, and spiritual growth. My goal is to provide a supportive space where clients can process these experiences with honesty, hope, and purpose.

As a Christian counselor, my work is both Christ-centered and person-centered. I honor each client’s unique story while integrating faith in a way that is meaningful and respectful. I enjoy working with individuals who desire counseling that aligns with their Christian beliefs and who are seeking deeper emotional healing, personal growth, and spiritual development.

I use a multimodal approach to counseling, drawing from evidence-based practices while also incorporating biblical principles when desired. This allows me to tailor therapy to each client’s needs — whether they are struggling with anxiety, life transitions, identity development, relationships, academic stress, or questions about faith and calling.

I am passionate about helping young adults build resilience, strengthen their identity, and develop confidence as they step into the future God is calling them toward. My hope is to create a warm, encouraging environment where clients feel seen, supported, and empowered during this important season of life.
